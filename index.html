What is a TFSA?

If you are Canadian and over 18, you should probably know what a TFSA (Tax Free Savings Account) is. It is an account that shields its contents from tax. You can have numerous financial instruments within the account and whatever gains are realized, they are tax-free. For example, if you own stocks inside your TFSA and their value increases, you will not be taxed on that income.
